Company Leave Policy (Sample Document)

1. Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year,
accrued monthly. Unused leave up to 5 days may be carried forward to the
next year.

2. Sick Leave
Employees may take up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical
certificate is required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

3. Work From Home
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval,
requested at least 24 hours in advance via the HR portal.

4. Notice Period
The standard notice period for resignation is 30 days for confirmed
employees and 15 days during the probation period.

5. Public Holidays
The company observes 12 public holidays per year, published annually on
the HR portal in January.
