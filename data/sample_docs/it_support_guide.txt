IT Support Guide (Sample Document)

1. Password Resets
Employees can reset their password via the self-service portal at
portal.company.local/reset. Resets via IT helpdesk require manager approval
and take up to 4 business hours.

2. VPN Access
VPN access is granted automatically to all full-time employees. Interns and
contractors must request VPN access through their manager, approval takes
1-2 business days.

3. Hardware Requests
New laptop requests go through the Asset Management system. Standard
turnaround is 5 business days; expedited requests require director approval.

4. Software Installation
Employees may self-install software from the approved catalog. Any software
outside the catalog requires a security review before installation.
